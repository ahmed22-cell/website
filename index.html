The Road to 5G and Beyond

Government initiatives and infrastructure upgrades are paving the way for a more sophisticated digital future, which will create new opportunities and shift consumer demand.

Now: Digital Transformation Strategy

Algeria is actively implementing its National Digital Transformation Strategy, aiming to train 500,000 ICT specialists by 2030 and build a robust local tech ecosystem.

Mid-2025: 5G Launch

The official launch of 5G services is set for Q3 2025, starting in major urban and industrial areas. This will unlock new possibilities for high-speed connectivity.

Future: Evolving Consumer Demand

As 5G becomes widespread, expect a surge in demand for 5G-compatible devices, high-performance accessories, and IoT products for a more connected lifestyle.
